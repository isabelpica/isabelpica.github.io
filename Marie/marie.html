This Information Graphic project is called “100 Comfy Things.” Created in Illustrator, this oversized poster is 2 feet wide and 4 feet tall. The project was assigned in Information Design and completed on February 26, 2020. Students were asked to collect 100 things and organize the information in a clear way for viewers to clearly understand the information. LATCH (Location, Alphabet, Time, Category, Hierarchy) was used to organize the content.
