Wedding Invitations

This is a wedding invitation that I have made for my future husband and I. I am currently engaged and I have made Invitations, Save The Date, and Thank You Notes for us to use for our wedding. I painted the Eucalyptus with watercolor and scanned it in the computer to add to the design. These were all created in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The Invitation and Save The Date cards are 7 inches tall and 5 inches wide and the Thank You Note is 3 inches tall and 5 inches wide.
