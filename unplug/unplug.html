Unplug

This Type as Image project is called “Unplug.” Created in Illustrator and Photoshop. The oversized poster is 24 inches long and 18 inches tall. This project was assigned in Typography 2 and was completed on April 23, 2019. Students were asked to pick a topic and a word that corresponds with it. An image was used to replace a letter in that word. This poster is about social media and how people should put down their phones and enjoy the world around them.
