What's The Temperature?

This Information Graphic project is called “What’s The Temperature?” Created in Illustrator and Photoshop. This oversized poster is 24 inches long and 18 inches tall. The project was assigned in Graphic Design 2 and completed on March 21, 2019. Students were asked to make an information graphic on the topic of their choice. A lot of research was involved in this project. This poster is about horses and what blood type category they fall under.
